    William M. Whelpley, in the 1860 Census, was living with his parents in Rye, Westchester, New York. During the Civil War, William enlisted May 20, 1861, as a volunteer Union soldier. He served in the 17th Regiment, New York infantry, Company B, as a sergeant. He also served in the 18th Regiment, New York National Guard, as a Second Sergeant.
    William married Elizabeth Carpenter on March 20, 1866, at Brooklyn, Kings Co., New York. In the 1880 Census, he was living in Olean, Cattaraugus County, New York with his wife and children, and was working as a painter.
